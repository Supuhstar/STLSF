Gateway to Space 2016

October 8, 2016

Innovations in Space


St. Louis Space Frontier is planning its Third Annual Gateway to Space Conference! The mission of Gateway to Space 2016 is to provide an opportunity for people immersed in the exploration, development, and settlement of Space to network and share innovative ideas, research, and products that will make an impact on thriving Space communities. The conference will be held at Saint Louis University's Parks College of Engineering, Aviation, and Technology on Saturday, October 8, 2016. For more information contact:  GatewaytoSpace@gmail.com.


The one day conference will include:

  • Morning coffee
  • Keynote presenters
  • Innovations in Space talks
  • Exhibits
  • Opportunities to network

In addition to the one day conference attendees may optionally participate in:
  • A Friday tour of Saint Louis University's Aerospace Engineering Labs, which include low speed and supersonic wind tunnels, a flow visualization water tunnel, 3-D printers, and the Space Systems Lab. Details coming soon!
  • Dinners on Friday and Saturday evening for networking fun.

Presentation Proposals

Are you interested in presenting YOUR idea, research, or product? If so, please submit an application using this link: http://goo.gl/forms/fc453SiYFc

Details: Presentations will be 25 minutes in length & we will attempt to accommodate all applications. Deadline for submitting abstract: September 1, 2016 at midnight CDT. Students, this is a great opportunity to practice presenting to an audience and to build your resume!
